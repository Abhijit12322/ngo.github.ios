"About Us

The foundation established by Rajesh Pradhan and Kanchan Subba in Agripam, Sonitpur, on December 4, 2020, addresses the critical issue of inadequate access to quality education among underprivileged children in India. Recognizing the significant disparities in educational opportunities, particularly in government schools where teaching quality and resources are often lacking, the foundation aims to uplift these children by providing comprehensive support. This includes free or subsidized coaching, essential study materials, practical and technical education, and computer literacy. By fostering holistic development through extracurricular activities and career guidance, the foundation seeks to empower children with the skills and knowledge necessary to overcome barriers and secure better futures. Through community engagement, partnerships, and teacher training, the foundation strives not only to improve educational outcomes locally but also to inspire broader societal change towards equitable education access across similar marginalized communities..

Aim

"To provide quality education and support to underprivileged children in rural areas, enhancing their skills and opportunities for a better future."

Mission

"Our mission is to bridge the educational gap for children in underserved communities by offering quality guidance, coaching, study materials, and practical learning opportunities. We focus on improving the quality of education in government schools and providing additional support in the form of technical and computer education. By empowering these children with the necessary skills and knowledge, we aim to foster their overall development and enable them to achieve their full potential."

Vision

"Our vision is a society where every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality education and the resources needed to succeed. We envision a future where all children in rural areas are equipped with the skills and knowledge to contribute to the growth and development of their communities and the nation."
