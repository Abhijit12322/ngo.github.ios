Our Story

CVE CARE FOUNDATION which stands for "Creative Vision Education". Its main objective is to provide "Quality Education" support to poor students.

CVE Care Foundation foundation was established on December 4, 2020, by two dedicated social workers, Rajesh Pradhan (Director) and Kanchan Subba (Director), in the remote village of Agripam in Sonitpur. The foundation was born out of their shared vision and commitment to addressing the educational disparities that hinder the growth and development of children in rural areas.

Education is undeniably vital for the progress of our nation. It is the cornerstone that shapes the future of our society, driving economic growth, social development, and individual empowerment. However, despite the constitutional mandate of the Right to Education, many children in our country remain deprived of "Quality Education" due to a lack of "Quality Guidance."

This problem is particularly acute among poor students whose parents are unable to afford tuitions and essential study materials. These financial constraints create a significant barrier to the children's ability to enhance their skills and compete on an equal footing with their more affluent peers.

While most children attend school under the Right to Education Act, the quality of education imparted, especially in government schools, often falls short. These institutions frequently lack the resources, trained teachers, and infrastructure necessary to provide a robust educational experience. As a result, students often miss out on the basic learnings and foundational skills that are crucial at their age, setting them back significantly in their educational journey.

Recognizing these challenges, our foundation is dedicated to bridging the gap and ensuring that every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality education and guidance. We aim to provide comprehensive support, including supplementary teaching, educational materials, and skill development programs. Our initiatives are designed to empower students, enhance their learning outcomes, and ultimately, contribute to the holistic development of the community.

By focusing on quality education and personalized guidance, we strive to create a more equitable educational landscape where every child has the opportunity to realize their full potential and contribute to the nation's growth and prosperity.
